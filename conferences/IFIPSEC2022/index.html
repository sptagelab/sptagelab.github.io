37th International Conference on ICT Systems Security and Privacy Protection – IFIP SEC 2022, 13–15 June 2022

Welcome to IFIP SEC 2022!

Welcome to the 37th International Conference on ICT Systems Security and Privacy Protection (SEC 2022) Web site. The SEC conferences are a series of well-established international conferences on Security and Privacy. SEC is the flagship event of the International Federation for Information Processing (IFIP) Technical Committee 11 (TC-11).

The IFIP SEC conferences aim to bring together primarily researchers, but also practitioners from academia, industry and governmental institutions to elaborate and discuss IT Security and Privacy Challenges that we are facing today and will be facing in the future. We seek submissions from academia, industry, and government presenting novel research on all theoretical and practical aspects of security and privacy protection in ICT Systems.

IFIP SEC 2022 will take place during 13 - 15 June 2022 and will be organized by the Cyber Security Section at Technical University of Denmark (DTU) in Copenhagen, Denmark.

Due to the ongoing pandamic, IFIP SEC 2022 will be conducted as a hybrid event!
