Getting here:

- By Air
Copenhagen International Airport is close by and just a 20-minute journey by taxi or there are regular direct trains to Copenhagen Central Station.

- By Car
We are conveniently located directly on the 02 Ring Road surrounding Copenhagen, with easy access from the rest of Denmark and Sweden. Public parking is available right outside the building, with fees payable at the parking meter.

- By Train
Copenhagen Central Railway Station is just an 8-minute walk or taxis and busses are always available outside.

- By Bus
Buses 5A, 30, 66 all stop near IDA Conference.

- By Bike
In Copenhagen cycling is one of the primary forms of transportation with an extensive network of dedicated lanes away from the roads. If you rent a bicycle during your stay, we also offer bicycle parking under our covered cycle parking area.

We strongly recommend that you use the planner to manage your trip: Planner.
