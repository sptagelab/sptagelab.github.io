Poster Submission

Poster Submission Guidelines

Each submission should contain a brief abstract, with an emphasis on the motivation for the work, and should describe the main contributions being presented.
Submission should only contain new and unpublished results and should point to the difference between the submission and any work under submission by the authors or any published work.

Submission will be evaluated on their potential to stimulate fruitful discussions, facilitate the exchange of ideas, and promote collaborations.

Poster proposals must be formatted according to the LNCS Proceedings Template and are limited to 5 pages, not including references.
Submissions are not anonymous and must contain the author's names and affiliations.
Accepted posters will be published in proceedings.
Submissions must be in PDF format and uploaded to the following EasyChair website:
https://easychair.org/my/conference?conf=esorics2022 (ESORICS 2022 - Posters)

Submissions that do not comply with the formatting guidelines will be rejected without review.

Questions shall be directed via E-mail to esorics2022posters@easychair.org
