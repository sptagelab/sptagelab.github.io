CPH Conference Centre

With only 50 meters to Copenhagen Central Station and 20 minutes to the airport, CPH Conference Centre is the great choice for you to have a great and confortable conference experience.

Not only the location is easy to reach, for anyone who drives, CPH Conference Centre offers free local car park with 192 parking spaces.

CPH Conference has been awarded a 5-star rating by HORESTA, thus classifying it as a conference centre that meets all 120 quality criteria in their points system. Furthermore, CPH Conference Centre's operator DGI Byen is an authorised Danish Conference Venues partner .

More Information

From CPH Lufthavn (Airport)

Since the CPH Conference Centre is situated beside Copenhagen Central Station (København H), reaching it from the airport is very straight forward. However, we suggest you to grab a travel card (Rejsekort) for a smoother public transportation experience.
You can take Trains at Terminal 2 and 3, while Buses only available at Terminal 2 and Metros only available at Terminal 3

Suggested Route for taking Public Transportation
  • Through Øresundsbanen (DSB Regional Train)
    • Regional Train / Øresundstoget (To: København H / Østerport / Nivå)
    • [København H]
  • Through Metro
    • Metro - M2 (To: Vanløse)
    • [Kongens Nytrov]
    • Metro - M3 / M4 (To: København H)
    • [København H]
  • By Bus
    • City Bus - 5C (To: Husum Torv / Herlev Hospital)
    • [Hovedbanegården, Tivolo (Bernstoffsgade)]
