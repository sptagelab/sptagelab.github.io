Registration

Register now (closed on 22 September), and get the early bird rate, please be advised the following regulation upon your registration:

  • At least one regular registration (e.g., A non-student registration) has to be made for Each accepted paper for Main Conference, Workshop, and Poster
  • For reduced student fee to apply, you must bring documentation (e.g., Student Card) and present this upon registration

In the registration page, you can find the following:

  • You can book the hotel rooms in the conference location. (26-30, Sep. with discount, First-Come-First-Reserved.)
  • You can also send a request to the Hotel extending the date to 25, Sep. if the room is available.
  • The early-bird registration for workshops has been extended to 10 September.
